Lasagna

description

a delicious family meal that both simple to make and easy to enjoy

ingredients

  1. 500g spagetti
  2. 2 tomatos
  3. 200g bacon
  4. 400g butter
  5. garlics

Steps

  • boil the pasta with small amount of water and salt then let it sit, this way the noddle will be chewy

  • now put butter in the hot pan, then fry the garlics till golden brown

  • now put in the chopped up tomatos, shimmers till sause texture, now put the spagetti in

  • lastly is the bacon, minder :don't have to be bacon you can put anytype of topping you want
